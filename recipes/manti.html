Description of Manti

Manti are small, spiced meat-filled dumplings popular in Turkish, Central Asian, and Caucasian cuisines. Typically steamed or boiled and topped with yogurt and a garlic or tomato-based sauce, theyâ€™re known for their delicate size and rich flavor.

Ingredients:

  • Dough (flour, water, salt)
  • Ground meat filling (usually beef or lamb, with onion and spices)
  • Yogurt-garlic sauce (plain yogurt mixed with minced garlic and salt)

Steps:

  1. Roll out dough thinly and cut into small squares.
  2. Fill each square with a bit of meat, fold into a dumpling, and seal.
  3. Boil or steam until cooked, then serve with yogurt-garlic sauce on top.
