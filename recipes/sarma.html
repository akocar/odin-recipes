Description of Sarma

Sarma is a traditional dish made of grape, cabbage, or other leafy vegetable leaves wrapped around a savory filling, typically of ground meat, rice, onions, and spices. It's popular in many Balkan, Middle Eastern, and Eastern European cuisines, and is usually slow-cooked in a tomato-based sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Sour cabbage leaves – for wrapping
  • Ground meat and rice mixture – the savory filling
  • Onion, garlic, and paprika – for flavor
  • Tomato sauce – for cooking and moisture

Steps:

  1. Sauté chopped onion and garlic, then mix with ground meat, rice, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Wrap spoonfuls of the mixture in cabbage leaves to form tight rolls.
  3. Layer rolls in a pot, optionally adding extra chopped cabbage or sauerkraut between layers.
  4. Pour tomato sauce and enough water to almost cover the rolls.
  5. Simmer covered on low heat for about 1.5 to 2 hours, until tender.
