Description of Manti

Sütlaç is a creamy, Turkish rice pudding made with rice, milk, sugar, and flavored with a touch of vanilla. It's typically served chilled, often topped with a caramelized sugar crust for extra sweetness and texture.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Milk
  • Sugar

Steps:

  1. Cook rice in water until tender, then drain.
  2. Simmer rice with milk and sugar, stirring constantly until thickened.
  3. Serve chilled, optionally caramelizing the top with sugar for a crispy finish.
